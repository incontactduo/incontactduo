Two musicians from Athens GR making music as a flute piano duo influenced by a broad spectrum of sounds and colors,
ranging from jazz to classical and from free improvisation to Greek traditional music with improvisation always
constituting the touchstone of their musical style.

Dimitris Verdinoglou     piano

Dimitris Verdinoglou was born in 1985 in Serres, Greece and commenced his journey in music by participating in Greek traditional music bands.

He studied at the Jazz Department of Ionian University (Corfu, Greece) with Lambis Vassiliadis (classical piano), Dimitris Kalantzis (jazz piano) and Dimos Dimitriadis (jazz and free improvisation). He graduated in 2010 obtaining a Bachelor’s degree on jazz piano while at the same time he received his Classical piano diploma.
He carried on with his studies at the Royal Conservatory of The Hague where he studied next to Jasper Soffers, John Ruocco, Eric Ineke etc. and graduated in 2013 receiving a Master’s Degree on solo jazz piano.

Dimitris has attended seminars and master classes with David Liebman, Brad Mehldau, Jeff Ballard, Danilo Pérez, John Patitucci, Brian Blade, Sheila Jordan etc. Furthermore, in 2008 he participated in the 18th meeting of the International Association of Schools of Jazz (IASJ) in Riga, Latvia.

From 2010 till 2016 he was an active member of the jazz scene of Holland and has participated in concerts, tours and festivals in Greece, Belgium, Ukraine, Holland and Lithuania. He has cooperated and performed with musicians such as: Benjamin Herman, Kike Perdomo, Panagiotis Andreou, Vasilis Xenopoulos, Dimitris Tsakas, Alexandros Ktistakis, Yiorgos Georgiadis, Vasilis Rakopoulos etc.

​Since October 2016 he has been living and working in Athens and is actively involved in the Greek jazz scene.

Leonidas Sarantopoulos     flute

Jazz flutist and composer Leonidas Sarantopoulos was born in Athens Greece in 1986. He started taking flute lessons at the age of 10 under Professor Raymond Maloli at the National Conservatory of Greece. He graduated from Pallini’s Music Middle and High School and the Music Department of the Ionian University in 2010 studying Jazz Flute with Professors Dimo Dimitriadi and Ria Georgiadou. Since then he lives and works in Athens, Greece participating actively in the jazz scene of Athens having performed in numerous music festivals and music scenes.

He has collaborated with many renowned Greek musicians, such as Vasilis Rakopoulos, George Kontrafouris, Nikos Sidirokastrtis, Stavros Lantsias, Antonis Andreou, Kostas Konstantinou, Andreas Poluzogopoulos etc. In addition, he has recorded two personal albums “Black Mamba” (2013) and “Paflax” (2017) and he has collaborated as a sideman, among others with the Arrabna project Grigoris Ntanis sextet, Thanos Hatzianagnostou quartet.
